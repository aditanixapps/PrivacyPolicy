Privacy Policy

SipBloom - Water Reminder
Effective Date: March 19, 2026

Introduction

SipBloom - Water Reminder ("SipBloom", "we", "our", or "us") is developed and published by Aditanix Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. SipBloom is designed with a privacy-first approach, and we believe your health data should remain yours alone.

Information We Collect

SipBloom stores all your personal health data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your health data on any external server.

Data stored locally on your device:

Data Type Purpose Stored Where
Water intake logs Track daily hydration Device only
Beverage selections Calculate hydration percentage Device only
Daily hydration goal Personalized target Device only
Profile data (weight, age, gender, activity level) Calculate recommended daily intake Device only
Streak and history data Track progress and provide insights Device only
Notification preferences Schedule reminders Device only

Data collected by third-party services:

Service Data Collected Purpose
Google AdMob Advertising ID, device info, approximate location Display relevant advertisements
Google Play Services Device identifiers, crash reports App distribution and stability

How We Use Your Information

Your locally stored data is used exclusively to provide the app's core functionality: tracking your water intake, calculating hydration goals, maintaining streaks, generating personalized insights, and scheduling reminder notifications.

We do not access, read, collect, or transmit any of your health data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Third-Party Services

SipBloom uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information and use your Advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information about how Google uses data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal health data with any third party. Your hydration data, profile information, and usage patterns are never transmitted from your device.

The only data shared with third parties is through Google AdMob for advertising purposes, as described above.

Data Security

All health data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage (Android Room Database and DataStore Preferences). The app includes security protections such as code obfuscation (R8/ProGuard), signature verification, and install source verification to prevent tampering.

Since your data is stored only on your device, it is protected by your device's own security features including screen lock, encryption, and biometric authentication.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. You can delete all your data at any time by:

1. Clearing app data: Settings → Apps → SipBloom → Storage → Clear Data

2. Uninstalling the app: This permanently removes all stored data

Since no data is stored on our servers, uninstalling the app completely removes all traces of your data.

Children's Privacy

SipBloom is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our target audience is users aged 18 and over. If you believe a child under 13 has used our app, please contact us and we will provide guidance on data removal.

No Account Required

SipBloom does not require you to create an account, sign up, provide an email address, or log in. The app works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection for its core functionality. Internet access is only used to display advertisements.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete all your data at any time through the app's settings or by clearing app data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information at all times.
