BloomHabit - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Introduction

BloomHabit ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by Aditanix Studios. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use BloomHabit - Habit Tracker.

Information We Collect

BloomHabit stores all your habit data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Habit names and settings you create

- Daily habit completion logs

- Streak and progress data

- App preferences and settings

Third-Party Services

BloomHabit uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted database. No data is uploaded to any server. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

Children's Privacy

BloomHabit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Permissions

BloomHabit requests the following permissions:

- Notifications: To send habit reminders at your chosen times

- Internet: Required for displaying advertisements

- Boot completed: To reschedule reminders after device restart

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
